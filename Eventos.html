Events

  • Art Night at "The Brush" Gallery

    Date and Time:Saturday, May 15th, 2024, from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM

    Location: "The Brush" Gallery, 123 Main Street, Creative City

    Event Description: Join us for an Art Night at "The Brush" Gallery. This special event will feature an exclusive exhibition of the latest works from our resident artists, as well as the unveiling of new pieces from emerging artists in the region. Immerse yourself in an evening of creativity and talent, with live music, drinks, and hors d'oeuvres to complement your art experience. Meet the artists behind the works, exchange ideas with fellow art enthusiasts, and discover that special piece that will brighten your home or office. Don't miss this opportunity to dive into the world of art and culture at our beautiful gallery!

    Cost and Registration: Admission is free. No registration is required. Just come and join us for a night filled with art and excitement.

  • Jazz Night Under the Stars

    Date and Time: Friday, June 3rd, 2024, from 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM

    Location: City Park Amphitheater, 456 Oak Street, Downtown

    Event Description: Join us for an enchanting evening of live jazz music at the City Park Amphitheater. Sit back, relax, and let the smooth sounds of jazz transport you under the twinkling stars. Featuring performances by renowned jazz artists from around the region, this event promises to be a night to remember. Bring your picnic blankets and baskets, gather with friends and family, and enjoy an unforgettable night of music and camaraderie in the heart of the city.

    Cost and Registration: Admission is free for all ages. No registration required. Simply arrive early to secure your spot and enjoy a magical night of jazz under the stars.
